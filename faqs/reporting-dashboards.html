Reporting & Dashboards

Common questions about running funder reports, using dashboards, customizing your homepage view, and exporting data from Mareto.

Reports Dashboards Data Export Funder Reporting
Q
How do I run a report for my funder?

Use the Filterable Reports tool. Select your filters — demographics, time period, program, status, or any captured data element — and export as CSV, PDF, or image. The Rollup Report feature is designed specifically for monthly funder submissions.

Tip: Because Mareto uses structured drop-downs rather than open text fields, your report data is clean and consistent from the moment it enters the system — no post-hoc cleanup required.
Q
What does my dashboard show?

Your dashboard is configured based on your role. A caseworker typically sees their caseload and upcoming appointments. A program manager sees program-level metrics such as enrollment, follow-up tracking, and discharge outcomes. Leadership sees the organizational dashboard with key metrics like total people served, open cases, referral volume, and available units.

Q
Can I customize what I see on my homepage?

Homepage views are configured per role during implementation. If you need changes to what appears on your dashboard, speak with your administrator — views can be adjusted to show the data most relevant to your position.

Q
How do I export data from Mareto?

From most report and list views, you can export as CSV, PDF, or image using the export button. Anything you put into Mareto, you can pull out — filter by any field, select your date range, and download.
