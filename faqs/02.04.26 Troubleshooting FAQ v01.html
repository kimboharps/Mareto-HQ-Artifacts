Troubleshooting

Quick answers to common issues you may encounter while using Mareto — from duplicate records to missing fields and access problems.

Duplicates Access Issues Missing Fields Support
Q
I think I created a duplicate record — what should I do?

Don't delete anything. Contact your administrator. They can use the file-merging tool to consolidate the duplicate records and preserve all associated cases, events, and history. No data will be lost in the merge.

Prevention tip: Always search for an existing person before creating a new record. The system uses fuzzy matching, so searching a first name or partial name will surface close matches.
Q
I can't see a client's record or certain fields — why?

Mareto uses role-based access controls that determine what each staff member can see. If a client is in a program outside your assigned access, their record or certain fields may not be visible to you. This is by design to protect client privacy.

Contact your administrator if you believe you should have access to a particular record or field.

Q
I need a field or drop-down option that doesn't exist — who do I ask?

Contact your administrator. Mareto is fully configurable, so new fields, drop-down values, event types, and workflows can be added as your organization's needs evolve. You can also submit a support ticket through the Help Centre.

Q
Something isn't working as expected — how do I get help?

Submit a support ticket through the Help Centre's Support Tickets tab. Include as much detail as possible — what you were trying to do, what happened instead, and any error messages you saw. The HelpSeeker support team will follow up.

For urgent issues, contact your administrator directly as they may be able to resolve configuration-related problems immediately.
