Surveys & External Data Collection

Common questions about sending surveys, creating external intake forms, and collecting documents from clients through Mareto.

Surveys Form Builder External Forms File Uploads
Q
How do I send a survey to clients?

Use the built-in Survey Tool. Create your survey, generate a link, and distribute it to participants. Responses are stored alongside case data — no need for a separate tool like SurveyMonkey. Automated follow-up surveys (e.g., 6 months post-referral) are also supported.

Q
How do I create an external form for intake or data collection?

Use the Form Builder to generate an external link. Share it on your website, with subcontractors, or with partner organizations. Submitted data flows directly into Mareto, and staff receive a notification when new submissions arrive.

Use cases: Website intake forms, subcontractor reporting, partner data submissions, and outcome measure collection can all be handled through external form links.
Q
Can I send clients a link to upload documents?

Yes. You can email participants links for file uploads — annual documents, consent renewals, or other required paperwork — directly through the platform. Uploaded files attach to the person's record.
