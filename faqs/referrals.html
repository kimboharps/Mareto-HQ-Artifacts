Referrals

Common questions about making internal referrals, receiving external referrals, and tracking referral outcomes in Mareto.

Internal Referrals External Referrals Referral Outcomes
Q
How do I make an internal referral to another program?

Use the Referral Management feature from the person's record or case. Internal referrals trigger notifications and emails to the receiving staff so they are aware of the incoming client.

Q
How do external referral forms work?

External partners (police, probation, partner agencies) submit referrals through a URL form. Submissions land in a separate holding area — they are not auto-created as person records. The system checks for name matches and flags potential duplicates before you process them.

Important: External referral submissions must be reviewed and approved before they become person records. This prevents accidental duplicates and ensures data quality.
Q
How do I track referral outcomes?

Each referral has an outcome field (successful, unsuccessful, pending). You can also view referral volume by source through your program dashboard, giving you a clear picture of where referrals are coming from and how they resolve.
